How to find the right product

  1. Step 1: Go to Categories tab and shop with categories

  2. Step 2: Select the category you want to shop with

  3. Or:

    Within the product section, click Chat seller to enquire more about the product

I can't make the right product selection

  1. Step 1: After selecting the category you want to shop in, then click the products displayed within that category

  2. Step 2: Above the product, click the Description tab to read the products description

  3. Repeat that process for the range of products displayed untill you find that of your satisaction

  4. Note:
    You can still Chat seller for more clarification.

How to know more about product

  1. Click the Description tab above the product for more product information

How do I contact seller

  1. Select the product and click the Chat seller button

How do I get a good seller

  1. Search for "Sellers" in the home page

  2. Check for sellers with ratings and the reviews made by fellow shoppers to broaden your horizon of seller selection

Why price sometimes changes at the checkout

  1. Prices change due to different currencies for different countries

  2. Price at the checkout will be calculated based on the currency of the country you chose during registration

  3. Note:
    While shopping be sure to select your country of residence

What is a wishlist

  1. Wishlist is where your favorite items go to.

    During shopping , you can come across a nice item that you intended not to buy, you can then put it in the Wishlist and get it next time
