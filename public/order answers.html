Place an order

  1. Step 1: Go to shopping categories and select category you want to shop in.

  2. Step 2: Select the item by clicking on it and select the option add to cart.

  3. Step 3: In the cart, select the option checkout, confirm your details and then click place order.

Pay for my order

  1. Step 1: From the categories tab, select item of your choice

  2. Step 2: Click the Add to cart button below the item

  3. Step 3: From the cart, click Checkout button that takes you to order confirmation page.

  4. Step 4: Click Place order button below and then select the payment mode that suits you best from the list

Track my order

  1. Step 1: Go to the Account page

  2. Step 2: Click In transit

  3. Step 3: Use the order number to track the location of the order

What's an order number

  1. A Fourteen-digit number that uniquely identifies each item purchased

Cancel my order

  1. Contact the customer support team on +254000000000

  2. If your order had been paid for, note that the refund processing time may vary depending on the payment method:
    • Card /bank transer - refund processing within 24 hours and may take 7-8 work days for it to reflect in your account
    • Mobile money - refund will be credited to the initial source within 24 hours
    • Voucher - voucher will be reactivated within 24 hours for use

    Our gratitude for your shopping with us


How do I know my order number

  1. Step 1: Select the item you want to purchase, add to cart

  2. Step 2: Checkout from the cart and confirm order by clicking the Place order button. The order number will be generated and check it at the top most of your order

Has my order arrived

  1. We shall send you a notification message to your phone number you provided us with.

    Furthermore keep tracking your order to know where your order has reached with time.
