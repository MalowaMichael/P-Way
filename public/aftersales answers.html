Goods not matching description

  1. Step 1: Go to Account tab, in the Aftersales section
  2. Step 2: Click To be reviewed to see the items that you ordered and their descriptions. Confirm the details.

    If the details in the delivered item do not match those in the ordered items, then click the Chat seller button below the item and do as instructed.

How do I return goods

  1. Step 1: Go to Account and to the Aftersales section
  2. Step 2: Select Apply refund/aftersale
