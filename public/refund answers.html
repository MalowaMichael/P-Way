Application for refund

  1. Step 1: Go to Account tab and in the Aftersales section

  2. Step 2: Select Apply refund/aftersales

  3. Step 3: Select items you want to return

  4. Step 4: Confirm Return order. You'll receive your refund after seller receives the items back

Refund not received yet

  1. Step 1: Account tab then in the Aftersales section

  2. Step 2: Click Processed to check items that their return process is completed.

    If there is an item in the processed section but you haven't received its refund, then go to chat us in the Ask tab to reach customer care agents for help

  3. Note:
    • Ensure to return the items back as instructed by the seller

    • You'll only be refunded after seller receives the items back in good condition

Duration of refund process

  1. The refund/aftersales process lasts for 7-8 work days
