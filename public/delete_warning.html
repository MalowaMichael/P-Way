Your Attention Please!

At signup, your account was created and your personal data generated then were stored with us. When you request for account deletion, you are about to erase all your personal data stored.

  • Please weigh your account deletion decision since it's ultimate and cannot be undone.
  • All your information; personal data and order information will be permanently deleted.
  • We'll no longer end you emails or notifications.
  • All unprocessed requests you applied for will be invalidated
  • P`Way will keenly verify your orders and account status before launching delete operation.
